* Ingredients

  * 2 - Waffles
  * 2 cups - Fresh or frozen strawberries
* Directions

  1. Toast waffles
  2. Pour syrup over waffles
  3. Cut strawberry leaves and tops off. Cut strawberries in half.

     Enjoy!
