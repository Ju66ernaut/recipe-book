* Ingredients

  * 2 - Waffles
  * 2 cups - Fresh or frozen strawberries
* Directions

  * Toast waffles
  * Pour syrup over waffles
  * Cut strawberry leaves and tops off. Cut strawberries in half.

    Enjoy!
